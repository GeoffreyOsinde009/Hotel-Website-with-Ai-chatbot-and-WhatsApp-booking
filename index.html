frequently asked questions

Is there cancellation fee?

Yes, there is a cancellation fee of 10% of the total booking amount for cancellations made within 24 hours of booking. Cancellations made more than 24 hours before check-in are free of charge.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, and American Express. We also support payments via PayPal and bank transfers.

What is the check-in and check-out time?

Check-in is from 3:00 PM, and check-out is until 12:00 PM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability.

Do you offer airport shuttle service?

Yes, we provide airport shuttle service for our guests. Please contact our concierge team in advance to arrange transportation.

Is there a fitness center on-site?

Yes, our hotel features a state-of-the-art fitness center equipped with modern exercise equipment for our guests to stay active during their stay.

Is breakfast included in the room rate?

Yes, breakfast is included in the room rate for all our guests. You can enjoy a delicious breakfast in our elegant restaurant.

How do I get breakfast?

Breakfast is served daily from 7:00 AM to 10:00 AM in the dining area and comes free with every room stay.

Is there a swimming pool available for guests?

Yes, we have a luxurious swimming pool where guests can relax and enjoy the beautiful surroundings.

Do you offer room service?

Yes, we provide 24/7 room service for our guests. You can enjoy delicious meals and beverages in the comfort of your room.
